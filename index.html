Biografia/Biography

Hey there! I'm Kirnir a Colombian electronic music producer and DJ. I'm passionate about creating unique rhythms that get people moving on the dance floor. My musical influences come from different genres and cultures, which allows me to create an authentic and diverse sound. My goal is to make people feel connected to the music and experience positive emotions through it. Thank you for listening to my music! If you want to know more about my upcoming projects, don't hesitate to follow me on my social media channels.
